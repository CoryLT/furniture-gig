## 4) Worker pay + 1099 tracking, off-platform (DONE — **SQL REQUIRED**)
- `supabase/worker_pay_tracking_20260617.sql` **must be run before deploy.** It:
  - adds `transactions.crew_member_id` (FK `crew_members`, on delete set null) + index;
  - creates the **`worker_payments` view** (security_invoker) — one row per expense
    txn tagged to a crew member: `txn_id, owner_user_id, crew_member_id, date,
    description, amount` (= sum of expense debit−credit). **This view is THE
    per-worker pay source now.**
  - extends `add_piece_expense` with `p_crew_member_id` (validates the crew member
    belongs to the operator, sets it on the txn). Named dollar tag `$ape$`.
- `app/flipper/pipeline/PipelineBoard.tsx`: when the expense category is **labor**,
  a "Who did you pay? (for 1099 tracking)" dropdown appears (the crew list). It
  passes `crewMemberId` into `add_piece_expense`, and after a tagged labor add it
  POSTs `/api/payments/check-1099` with `{ crewMemberId }`.
- `app/flipper/pipeline/page.tsx`: loads `crew` (id + label — on-platform names via
  `worker_profiles`, off-platform via `worker_name`) and passes it to the board.
- `app/flipper/records/page.tsx` (Payment Records) **rewritten**: reads
  `worker_payments` grouped by crew member per year, names from `crew_members`
  (+`worker_profiles`), name links to `/flipper/crew/[id]`, 1099 flag ($600 ≤2025,
  $2,000 for 2026+), CSV export. No longer touches `gig_payments`.
- `app/api/payments/check-1099/route.ts` **rewritten**: body `{ crewMemberId }`,
  sums `worker_payments` for that member in the current year, fires once at/over the
  threshold (dedup on `notifications.data->>crew_member_id` + year), notification
  `actor_user_id = null` with `data { crew_member_id, worker_name, year, total }`,
  plus push + email.
- `components/shared/NotificationBell.tsx`: the `1099_threshold` case now reads
  `data.worker_name` (there's no on-platform actor anymore).
- **Usage:** add the worker to My Crew (off-platform, name-only is fine) → log their
  pay as a Labor expense on the piece → tag them in the dropdown. Untagged labor
  still counts as the piece's cost but won't show per-worker. The dropdown only
  lists people already in My Crew.
